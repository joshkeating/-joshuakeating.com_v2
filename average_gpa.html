Average Gpa Professor Picker

December 19, 2016

Overview

This is a small utilitarian web application for UW students to compare the average GPA given by professors year over year. Based on the class and year selected it arranges professors in descending order by average GPA. I built it using R, plotly, and shiny. Check out the site here and the source here.
